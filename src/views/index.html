Baduk

The easiest Go platform you've ever used.

What do we do?

Baduk is a Go platform without all the bullshit of traditional websites. Developed by a group of students at the University of Waterloo who are sick of ads, sign-up processes and e-mails. We'll make sure you enjoy Go the way its meant to be.

Ad-Free

We know that ads are the last thing you wanna see when playing a game, so we refuse to have them. Enjoy a distraction-free environment that no other Go site can provide.

Ready To Go

With one click you will get a unique link to share to play with your friends. Skip the sign in bullshit completely and head stright into game mode.

Fluid UI

Everything is done with the latest coding paradigms, everything is pretty and clean. Above all, its fast as hell.

Free games? What's the catch?

As gamers ourselves, we're sick and tired of gaming sites having countless ads, sign-up processes, e-mail verification, and a tonne of other annoying site-related obstacles. We just want to play our damn games. Inspired by the great Lichess, we will never have any of the aforementioned annoyances.

About Baduk

We're a bunch of students at the University of Waterloo, studying computer science. Baduk is built using Node.js and Socket.io. If you'd like to give back, you can help contribute to the site. Baduk is an open-source project, so feel free to submit a feature or an issue to our repository.
