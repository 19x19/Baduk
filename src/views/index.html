What is Baduk?

Baduk is a Go platform without all the bullshit of traditional websites. Developed by a group of students at the University of Waterloo who are sick of ads, plugin downloads, sign-up processes and e-mails. We'll make sure you enjoy Go the way its meant to be enjoyed.

Ad-Free

We know that ads are the last thing you wanna see when playing a game, so we promise you an ad-free environment.

No Sign-Up

With one click you will get a link to share with your friends. Skip the sign-in completely and head straight into game mode.

Open Source

Created using the most bleeding-edge technology stack, we welcome any programmers that want to contribute to our repository.

About Baduk

Baduk is created by a group of students at the University of Waterloo. It's built using Node.js and Socket.io. If you'd like to give back, you can help contribute to the site. Baduk is an open-source project, so feel free to submit a feature or an issue to our repository.

How We Benefit

Baduk is not for profit. We don't have ads and we don't collect your information. We cover all the costs of Baduk using our own savings. Inspired by the great Lichess, we want to do the world a small favour by offering a free service.
